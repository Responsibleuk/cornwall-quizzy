Experience Cornwall

Our digital platform will bring together a network of peers in which the our members will interact and share in the value creation of our project. Members will deliver experiences, build relationships, share advice, collaborate, co-create and much much more.

Experience Cornwall allows users to search for experiences in their local area, see availability in real time and book. Find, book, do. From art classes to zip-line rides, Experience Cornwall is to experiences what AirBnB is to accommodation.

What makes Experience Cornwall unique is we are a social enterprise, all of our profits will be reinvested to help improve the businesses of our community members.
